Welcome to the first post on **MIKESTH3TIC.DEV**! 

Building a personal platform in 2024 requires more than just a list of projects. It requires a statement of intent. My goal for this site was to build something that feels like a startup product: high performance, interactive, and beautifully engineered.

## The Tech Stack

I chose a stack that allows for fast iteration without sacrificing user experience:

- **Next.js 14 (App Router)**: The gold standard for modern React applications.
- **Tailwind CSS**: For rapid, consistent styling.
- **Framer Motion**: To bring the UI to life with high-fidelity animations.
- **Firebase**: For real-time functionality and effortless authentication.
- **Three.js**: To add that futuristic, "high-tech" feel to the hero section.
